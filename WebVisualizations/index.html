Summary: Latitude VS. X

Latitude of the project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherApi to assemble a dataset on over 500 cities. After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot the various aspects of the weather vs. the latitude.

Factors we looked at included: wind speed, and humidity. This site provides the source data visulasitons created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
